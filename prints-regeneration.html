Continuous Cities 5 (Calvino), 2006
Embossed etching from Braille Suite
46 cm x 54.5 cm

Braille Suite is one work within the Regeneration project.  It is a set of five individual texts extracted from Italo Calvino’s Invisible Cities which have then been transcribed into Braille and printed as blind embossed etchings.  Presented in a language of touch rather than sight, and yet unreadable behind glass, they make the cities they describe inaccessible to us: at once found and lost.
